Join Vardān

We have named our Annual Giving programme, Vardān (boon or blessing) because it is not only about financial support - it is an opportunity to engage with a dynamic creative community and share key moments in our artistic lives and processes.

Vardān offers our supporters a unique opportunity to become an integral part of the Nrityagram community by funding key initiatives from village outreach, to training the next generation of performers and teachers, to creating new works of art. It allows every single person to participate through a wide range of pledge levels, with wonderful benefits to everyone.
