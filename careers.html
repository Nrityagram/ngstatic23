Job Opportunities

The opening for the post of Communications Associate is now closed. If you think you have skills that will enrich our organization, please email us at jobs@nrityagram.org


Communications Associate

Nrityagram is looking for a Communications Associate to join the team responsible for growing and sustaining the organization.

As Communications Associate, you will plan and implement public relations and marketing activities and will work to continually raise the visibility of Nrityagram in the community through public relations, including but not limited to the website and social media.

You will also be responsible for actively supporting the fundraising activities of Nrityagram. This includes, but is not limited to assisting in the annual appeal, major Endowment solicitation, writing grant proposals and planning events.

An integral and essential part of your role is the development of effective, targeted communications to all stakeholder groups (volunteers, friends, donors, members, and audience), continually seeking to inform and inspire.


Essential Duties and Responsibilities

Communications & Public Relations

  • Plan and implement communications strategies
  • Draft and edit communications copy (e.g. Emails, Publications, E-Newsletter, Blog, Social media posts, Press releases, etc.)
  • Develop and execute social media strategies

Fund Raising

  • Produce donor and special category solicitations/support materials with the intent to retain or upgrade gifts when possible, and all printed material inventory required for fundraising
  • Produce specialised correspondence, letters of acknowledgment, and materials for donors
  • Interface with fundraising team to fulfil information requests
  • Prepare presentations and reports

Proposal Writing

  • Solicit and write proposals and reports for government, foundation and corporate funds

Ideal Experience and Qualifications

  • Excellent written and oral communication skills that are effective with a diverse range of audiences including board members, volunteers, internal staff, donors, executives, potential funding partners and all constituencies; produce compelling and donor-centric communications and articulate impact to supporters
  • Ability to convey a high degree of commitment and passion for the mission of Nrityagram
  • Ability to maintain a high level of confidentiality
  • High energy, positive, “can-do” attitude, flexibility, teamwork, and attention to detail; high degree of initiative
  • Understanding of Digital media and Social media strategies
  • Strong computer skills and proficient with Word Press and MS Office programs
  • Familiarity with design software (e.g. Photoshop, InDesign) and content management systems is a plus
  • BSc/BA in Marketing, Communications or a related field is desired
  • Experience as a Communication Specialist or similar role is a plus
  • Interest / involvement with the performing arts is a big plus
  • You must be prepared to make at least a 1-year commitment to this position

Compensation and Benefits

  • Nrityagram is a not-for-profit organisation that enjoys great goodwill across the globe, built over 3 decades of practice, performance and outreach. We are constrained in the ability to offer market-driven compensation but guarantee a rich and memorable experience and immense satisfaction.
  • Position is part-time with flexi-hours and you can work remotely
  • Compensation is ₹10,000-20,000 (commensurate with experience) with additional benefits

How to Apply

Email your resume and a cover letter detailing your interest in this job to lynne@nrityagram.org
