Quiet Space

In these troubled times, our spirit is exhausted and we seek solace. We want to rest under the shade of a quiet moment. To find comfort and strength in community and shared experiences.

So, we created Quiet Space where twice a day, we can gather together and listen to music - multi-faith devotional music, songs of hope, music to soothe our souls, perhaps even birdsong from Nrityagram - anything that will bring us peace and courage. A Circle of Solace for all of us.

These are the timings, though the music plays through the day:

India 8am - 11am 5pm - 8pm
Perth 10.30am - 1.30pm 7.30pm - 10.30pm
London 3.30am - 6.30am 12.30pm - 3.30pm
EDT 10.30pm - 1.30am 7.30am - 10.30am

We invite you to join Quiet Space as often as you can. Come in at your convenience and leave when your heart tells you to.

It is our small attempt to stitch together a quilt, and take shelter awhile.
