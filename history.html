History
  • 9 May 1979

    Odissi Dance Centre Trust is incorporated
    Nrityagram is a project of the Odissi Dance Centre Trust

  • 1989

    Protima Gauri (Gaurima) starts building Nrityagram

  • 11 May 1990

    Nrityagram is inaugurated by the Prime Minister of India

  • 1990

    Odissi Gurukul is completed

  • February 1990

    Pavithra Reddy joins the Village Outreach programme

  • 20 June 1990

    Surupa Sen joins as a residential student

  • February 1991

    Vasantahabba, an all-night Spring Festival is launched

  • 1992

    Mohiniattam Gurukul is completed

  • 1993

    The Nrityagram Dance Ensemble is formed

    Bijayini Satpathy joins the Ensemble

    Lynne Fernandez joins as Technical Director

  • September 1993

    Nrityagram’s first International tour as Protima Bedi and Party

  • 24 September 1994

    The Nrityagram Dance Ensemble makes its National debut in Delhi

  • 1995

    Kathak Gurukul is completed

  • 22 March 1996

    Surupa’s solo debut

  • 18 June 1996

    New York debut of the Nrityagram Dance Ensemble

  • 5 March 1997

    Bijayini’s solo debut

  • 14 June 1997

    Protima officially hands over Nrityagram to Lynne

  • July 1997

    Surupa appointed Artistic Director, Nrityagram
    Bijayini appointed Director of the Odissi Gurukul

  • 18 August 1998

    Gaurima dies in a landslide, while on a pilgrimage to Kailash Mansarovar
    Surupa and Bijayini’s first group of students join Nrityagram

  • July 1999

    Nrityagram receives a National Dance Project Grant to make Śrī In Search of the Goddess
    Full-Time Musicians come to stay and work

  • December 1999

    Surupa works for the first time with Pt. Raghunath Panigrahi
    A very successful collaboration lasting until Panditji’s passing in 2013

  • June 2000

    Surupa receives an Asian Cultural Council grant to attend the International Choreographers Residency at the American Dance Festival

  • 3 May 2001

    Premiere of Śrī: In Search of the Goddess

  • 2003

    Bharatnatyam Gurukul is started
    Discontinued in 2006

  • 27 December 2003

    Pavithra’s solo debut

  • 2 January 2005

    Premiere of Ansh

  • 12-17 April 2005

    Nrityagram’s debut season at The Joyce Theater, New York

  • July 2005

    Nrityagram receives a National Dance Project Grant to make Sacred Space

  • 19 December 2005

    Premiere of Sacred Space

  • January 2006

    Surupa receives the Raza Foundation award

  • 19-23 July 2006

    Nrityagram’s debut Season at Jacobs Pillow Dance Festival

  • 23 March 2007

    Bijayini is awarded Sangeet Natak Akademi’s Ustad Bismillah Khan Yuva Puraskar

  • May 2007

    The Joyce Theater, NY commissions 25 choreographers to create work for its twenty-fifth Anniversary - Surupa commissioned to make Pratimā: Reflection

  • 19 February 2008

    Premiere of Pratimā: Reflection

  • 6 July 2008

    Surupa and Bijayini receive the Yagnaraman award for Excellence in Dance from Sri Krishna Gana Sabha, Chennai

  • 5 November 2010

    Kula - Residence for Artists is completed

  • 11 November 2010

    Premiere of Śriyaḥ

  • 9 December 2011

    Surupa and Bijayini receive the Nritya Choodamani from Sri Krishna Gana Sabha, Chennai

  • 24 February 2012

    Premiere of Saṃhāra

  • 25 August 2012

    Premiere of Saṃyoga

  • 18 August 2013

    First NrityaUtsarga, our Annual School Show

  • 22 November 2013

    Premiere of Songs of Love and Longing

  • 17 August 2014

    Debut performance of Nrityagram Two

  • 11 JAN 2015

    Saṃyoga at Temple of Dendur, MET Museum, New York NY

  • 11 May 2015

    Nrityagram turns 25!

  • March 2016

    Surupa is Resident Artist at Baryshnikov Arts Center, New York

  • 13 September 2016

    Australia debut of the Nrityagram Dance Ensemble

  • 19 May 2017

    Revival of Saṃhāra

  • 27 August 2017

    Premiere of Yadunandana

  • 27 October 2018

    Saṃhāra at the MET Museum, New York

  • March 2019

    Food Foresting begins at Nrityagram

  • May 2019

    Pavithra creates the NG101 programme

  • 16 July 2019

    Surupa is awarded the Sangeet Natak Akademi Award for Odissi dance

  • 20 September 2019

    Premiere of Āhuti

  • April 2020

    Classes go online

  • 1 May 2020

    Nrityagram Live: From our Studio to your Home

  • 11 May 2020

    Nrityagram is 30!

  • 18 August 2020
